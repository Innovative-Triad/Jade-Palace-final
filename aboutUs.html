About Us

Jade Palace, a contemporary gem, made its grand entrance in June 2022 and marks the first standalone venture by Chef-Owner Li Wei (formerly of Hakkasan and Tao) and Partner-Operator Mei Ling (formerly of The Nomad and Eleven Madison Park). Nestled at the 500 Terry Francine Street, San Francisco, Jade Palace exudes the elegant charm of a modern Asian bistro. Housed in a beautifully restored historic building, the restaurant offers a menu that seamlessly blends traditional Asian flavors with a modern twist, featuring dim sum, fresh seafood, and signature dishes like Peking Duck, Spicy Sichuan Noodles, and Miso Black Cod. Standout options include the Crispy Duck Salad, Wagyu Beef Bao, and the indulgent Seafood Hot Pot. Don't miss the Dessert Cart, which offers a delightful array of Asian-inspired sweets. The sophisticated space, crafted by renowned designer Ken Fulk, is unified by the central open kitchen, a captivating centerpiece that invites both locals and visitors to experience the vibrant heart of Jade Palace.
